Port of Beira
The Port of Beira, located in the city of Beira in Mozambique, is one of the country's main logistical and strategic centers,
playing a key role in regional and international trade. Situated on the coast of the Indian Ocean, this port
serves as an important outlet for exports and imports from Mozambique and inland countries such as Zimbabwe, Zambia and Malawi.
